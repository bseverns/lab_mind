# Portainer

## Role
Portainer is the local stack control room.

Use it for:
- checking whether containers are up
- restarting a service
- inspecting logs
- confirming env or bind mounts

## Do not use it for
- inventing architecture under pressure
- changing five services at once
- pretending logs are the same thing as diagnosis

## Day-1 check
Confirm:
- Portainer opens
- endpoint is healthy
- stack is visible
- core containers are present

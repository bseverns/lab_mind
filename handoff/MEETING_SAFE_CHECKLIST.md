# Meeting-Safe Checklist

Use this when the lead operator is away.

## Do first
- boot Jetson-A
- confirm network
- confirm NVMe exists
- confirm Docker works
- open browser cockpit
- open Digital Factory

## Do not do
- do not invent Headscale values
- do not rename printers just to see what happens
- do not expose services publicly
- do not attach Cubiko unless it is part of the plan
